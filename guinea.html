Guinea Categories

Meet the charming and sociable guinea breeds, small rodents prized for their friendly personalities, gentle nature, and expressive vocalizations. Each breed varies in coat type and appearance, from the smooth-coated American to the long-haired Peruvian. Guineas are ideal pets for both beginners and experienced owners, as they require minimal space and simple care routines. Their curious nature and ability to recognize owners make them delightful companions. They thrive best in pairs or groups, enjoying social interaction and gentle handling. Guinea breeds offer warmth and joy to homes with their engaging behaviors and endearing sounds.

American

Short, smooth coat, and very friendly.

Abyssinian

Has rosettes and a rough coat.

Peruvian

Long-haired and needs regular grooming.

Silkie

Silky coat and gentle temperament.

Teddy

Dense, springy coat like a teddy bear.
